Mission Statement

There are so many things vying for our attention these days: phones,

tablets, TVs, laptops. It's hard to find a moment to just be present.

But it's even harder for our kids. They're growing up in a world where

technology is everywhere, and it's hard for them to find a break from

it all. That's why I created the Armando Promise: a series of apps that

are free from ads, in-app purchases, and distractions. I want kids to be

able to enjoy their childhood and be present in the moment.

I want them to be able to use technology: mindfully.
